Searching for Datasets

This dataset shows the number of coal miners employed in the United States, by month, from 1985 to 2019. I hope to demonstrate the rate at which coal mininers have decreased in the United States over time and reveal patterns in this change.

This dataset shows economic information about Appalachian counties. It includes data about the unemployment rates, per capita market incomes, and poverty rates. I hope to show these numbers in comparison to state and national averages which are also included in the CSV to reveal information about the Appalachian region.
